PyCascades

Community PyCon for the PNW

PyCascades is a regional Python software conference in the Pacific Northwest, celebrating the west coast Python developer and user community.

Organized by members of the Python communities in and around Vancouver, Seattle, and Portland, we aim to bring together Python users and developers from both the Cascade Region and around the world.

The conference occurs each year around early spring, and rotates annually between Vancouver, British Columbia, Seattle, Washington, and Portland, Oregon.
